### Typography
- **Headings:** Manrope — Bold. H1: 60–64px, H2: 42–48px, H3: 28–32px
- **Body:** Inter — Regular. Body: 18px, Small: 15–16px
- **Fallback:** Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif

### Visual Style
- Light background (`#F8FAFC`) as the dominant canvas — this is an enterprise consulting site, not a SaaS startup
- Premium whitespace — generous padding, never cluttered
- Card-based layouts with subtle shadows and clean borders
- Soft scroll-reveal animations only — no heavy motion or particle effects
- Large executive photography (boardroom settings, professionals, AI/data overlays)
- Business-first line iconography — not emojis
- Royal blue (`#295BFF`) as the primary CTA and accent colour
- Dark navy sections used sparingly for contrast (hero accents, CTA blocks, footer)
- Overall tone: McKinsey meets AI-native firm — trusted, institutional, forward-looking

## Design Guidelines

**Do:**
- Match the provided reference screenshot faithfully for layout and section structure
- Use light `#F8FAFC` background as the dominant page canvas
- Keep photography large and executive-quality (placeholder images fine for now)
- Make every page feel like it belongs alongside BCG.com or Accenture.com
- Use consistent card spacing, shadow depth, and border radius throughout

**Avoid:**
- Dark backgrounds as the dominant theme — light is the base, dark is the accent
- Particle effects, animated gradients, or any tech-startup visual clichés
- Cluttered layouts — whitespace is a design element, not empty space
- Neon colours or anything that reads as "SaaS product" rather than "consulting firm"
